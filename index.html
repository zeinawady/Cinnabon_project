Our Story

Cinnabon is the worldwide leader in the cinnamon roll bakery category. Founded in Seattle, Washington, In 1985, Cinnabon opened its first bakery on December 4, 1985, at Sea Tac Mall. The brand has grown dramatically since then. As of July 2005, the company had over 600 bakeries worldwide. These bakeries are located in 30 countries, leading to exceptional brand awareness worldwide.
